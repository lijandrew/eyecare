About Jennifer Wei Liao, M.D.

Jennifer Wei Liao, M.D., is a board-certified ophthalmologist who has been serving the Queens community with over 20 years of experience... Dr. Liao provides comprehensive ophthalmology services from her modern office in the SkyView Wellness state-of-the-art facility, which is located in the shops at SkyView shopping mall.Dr. Liao graduated from SUNY Stony Brook Medical School and completed her residency training through New York University at North Shore-LIJ Health Systems (now Northwell Health). Dr. Liao then taught surgery and ophthalmology to new physicians as an Associate Professor at New York University. Dr. Liao is known throughout the Flushing community as a thorough, meticulous and caring physician who puts her patients first.
